🏠 Home
/

Purpose: First impression for new visitors and returning guests. Drive traffic to Menus, Catering, and Private Parties. Establish brand warmth and Maple Grove community identity.

Sections to Feature:

  • Hero: Full-width image/video of the restaurant atmosphere. Headline reinforcing the brand ("Great food, cold drinks, good people") with CTAs to Menus and Reservations.
  • Featured Menu Highlights: Preview of 3-4 signature dishes or seasonal items with photos.
  • Catering & Private Parties Callout: Two-up card section promoting both services with CTAs.
  • About Snippet: Short brand story blurb with link to full History page.
  • Rewards Program Promo: Sign-up teaser with loyalty program benefits.
  • Hours & Location: Quick reference block with address, hours, and embedded map.
